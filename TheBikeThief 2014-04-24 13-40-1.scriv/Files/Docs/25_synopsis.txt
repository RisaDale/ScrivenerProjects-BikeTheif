Ellenbogen is trying to destroy history - Rando is trying to save it 



This is about caverns filled with perpetual motion machines and battled over by Philosoraptors and Yeti's with Yumanity taking a sideline. Bikes figure in heavily- all the wheels become cogs in the machine, making sure the steam powered dream chipper sends shards of common sense into a world that is run by a man who is frightened of dogs
